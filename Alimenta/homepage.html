About Us

Alimenta is a non-profit organization dedicated to eradicating hunger through innovative programs and community support initiatives.

Mission and Vision

Our mission is to eliminate hunger by providing meals, resources, and hope to individuals and families in need. Our vision is to create a world where no one goes to bed hungry.

Our Story

Founded in 2018, Alimenta was a small community project in Malaysia and has since grown to serve communities across South East Asia. Our efforts are driven by the belief that hunger is a solvable problem.

Our Team

Our team consists of passionate individuals, including volunteers, donors, and staff, all united by a common goal: to end hunger and make a lasting impact in the communities we serve.
